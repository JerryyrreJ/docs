Bark is a free iOS app that receives push notifications from any service you configure. Sub integrates with Bark to deliver renewal reminders even when the app is closed — a server-side scheduled function handles delivery, so your device always gets the alert at the right time regardless of whether you have Sub open.

## Prerequisites

Complete both of the following before configuring Bark in Sub.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Install Bark on iOS" icon="mobile">
    Download [Bark](https://bark.day.app) from the App Store on your iPhone or iPad. Once installed, open the app to reveal your unique device key and server URL.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Sign in to Sub" icon="cloud">
    Sign in to Sub with a cloud account. Notification settings are stored in the cloud, so an active session is required for reminders to work.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Configure Global Bark Settings

Follow these steps to enable Bark notifications for your Sub account.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Notification Settings">
    Launch Sub, open the user menu, and select **Notification Settings**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enable Bark push notifications">
    Toggle **Bark push notifications** on. This is the global switch that controls whether Sub can send any Bark reminders at all.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your Bark server URL">
    Type your Bark server URL into the **Server URL** field. Use `https://api.day.app` for the free official server, or enter your own self-hosted URL if you run a private Bark instance.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your Bark device key">
    Open the Bark app on your iOS device — your device key is displayed on the home screen. Copy it and paste it into the **Device Key** field in Sub.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set days before renewal">
    Enter a number in the **Days before renewal** field. For example, entering `3` tells Sub to notify you 3 days before each upcoming payment date.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save settings">
    Tap or click **Save**. Sub stores your settings in the cloud so your reminders are ready to fire on schedule.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Enable Notifications Per Subscription

The global Bark toggle arms the system, but each subscription also has its own notification switch. Turn it on for every subscription you want to receive reminders about.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open a subscription">
    From your subscription list, tap or click the subscription you want to configure.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enable the notification toggle">
    Find the **Notification enabled** toggle and switch it on.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save the subscription">
    Save your changes. The subscription is now scheduled to trigger a Bark push the configured number of days before its next renewal.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Both the global Bark toggle **and** the per-subscription toggle must be on for a reminder to fire. Enabling one without the other results in no notification being sent.
</Warning>

## Official vs. Self-Hosted Bark Server

Choose the Bark server option that best fits your setup.

| Option | URL | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Official server | `https://api.day.app` | Free, maintained by the Bark team, no setup required |
| Self-hosted | Your own URL | Full control over your data; Docker image available from the Bark project |

<Note>
  Reminders are delivered by a server-side scheduled function. You do not need Sub open on any device for reminders to fire — set it up once and let the scheduler handle the rest.
</Note>

<Tip>
  If you stop receiving reminders, the first thing to check is that both the global Bark toggle in Notification Settings **and** the per-subscription toggle are enabled.
</Tip>

## Troubleshooting

<Accordion title="Why am I not receiving notifications?">
  Work through the following checklist to identify the cause.

  - **Global Bark toggle is off** — Open **Notification Settings** and enable Bark push notifications.
  - **Per-subscription toggle is off** — Edit the subscription and switch the **Notification enabled** toggle on.
  - **Wrong device key** — Open the Bark app on your iOS device and copy the key directly from the home screen. Paste it into the **Device Key** field in Sub.
  - **Wrong server URL** — Verify that the URL you entered is reachable. If you self-host Bark, confirm the server is running and accessible from the public internet.
  - **Account not signed in** — Notifications require a cloud account. Sign in to Sub and confirm your session is active before expecting reminders to fire.
</Accordion>
