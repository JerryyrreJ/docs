## What Sub does

Sub tracks recurring payments with category, amount, currency, billing period, and renewal date. A live currency converter lets you view all spending in one base currency. Built-in analytics surface your top subscriptions, category breakdowns, and upcoming renewal calendar.

## Get started in three steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the app">
    Go to [sub.jerrylu.app](https://sub.jerrylu.app). No sign-up required — Sub works entirely in your browser by default.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add a subscription">
    Click **Add subscription**, fill in the name, amount, currency, billing period, and last payment date. Sub calculates the next renewal date automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Explore your dashboard">
    Switch between monthly and yearly views, change your base currency, and open **Analytics** to see where your money is going.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Sign in with a Supabase-backed account to sync across devices and enable server-side renewal reminders. See [Account & Cloud Sync](/account-and-sync) to get started.
</Tip>
